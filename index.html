About ACZ

A Cold Zobo was founded in 2022 to help Nigerians enjoy the benefits of Zobo prepared with good health in mind. Since then, we have opened kiosks in major cities including Lagos, Abuja, Ekiti and Ogun state.

Zobo Trivia

Who should not be drinking Zobo?

Despite the health benefits , Zobo cannot be enjoyed by everyone. Pregnant and breastfeeding women should avoid it, as it could cause miscarriages and the effects on breastfeeding women are unknown. People with low blood pressure should also avoid Zobo, or consult their doctor before taking the drink.
